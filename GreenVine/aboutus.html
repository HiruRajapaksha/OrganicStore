About GreenVine Market

We're more than just a grocery store. We're a community of passionate individuals committed to sustainable living, supporting local farmers, and bringing the freshest organic produce to Bath families.

Our Mission

To create a sustainable food system that nourishes our community while protecting the environment. We connect conscious consumers with local organic farmers, ensuring that every purchase supports both personal health and planetary wellness. Through transparency, quality, and community partnership, we're building a future where healthy food is accessible, affordable, and environmentally responsible.

Our Core Values

🌍

Environmental Stewardship

We believe in protecting our planet for future generations through sustainable farming practices, minimal packaging, and carbon-neutral delivery options.

🤝

Community Support

Supporting local Bath area farmers and producers is at the heart of what we do. We ensure fair prices and long-term partnerships that benefit everyone.

💚

Health & Wellness

We are committed to providing the freshest, most nutritious organic produce to help our customers live healthier, more vibrant lives.

🔄

Circular Economy

From compostable packaging to zero-waste initiatives, we strive to create a closed-loop system that minimizes environmental impact.

Our Eco-Friendly Practices

📦

Sustainable Packaging

All our packaging is either recyclable, compostable, or reusable. We use minimal packaging and encourage customers to return containers for reuse.

🚲

Carbon-Neutral Delivery

Our delivery fleet includes electric vehicles and bicycles for local deliveries, minimizing our carbon footprint while serving the community.

🌱

Organic Certification

We work exclusively with certified organic farmers who use sustainable, chemical-free growing methods that protect soil health and biodiversity.

💧

Water Conservation

Our partner farms employ water-saving irrigation techniques and rainwater harvesting to minimize water usage and protect local watersheds.

♻️

Zero Waste Goal

We compost all organic waste, donate imperfect produce to local food banks, and continuously work toward eliminating waste in our operations.

🏪

Local Sourcing

Over 80% of our products come from within 50 miles of Bath, reducing transportation emissions and supporting the local economy.
